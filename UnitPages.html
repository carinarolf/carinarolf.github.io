7th Grade Math
Units of Study

Each unit of study below includes a page of vetted resources as well as an example lesson plan that can be administered as-is, or augmented to best support your students.

NDE State Standards Common Core Alignment Study MPS iMII Resource Folder

UNIT 1: Integer Operations
~15 days

REVIEW: fraction operations, decimal operations, order of operations, exponents, and absolute value.

FOCUS: addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division with integers.

Resources Example Lesson Plan

UNIT 2: Rational Number Operations
~20 days

UNDER CONSTRUCTION

REVIEW: fraction operations, decimal operations.
FOCUS: ordering & comparing rational numbers, adding & subtracting rational numbers, multiplying & dividing rational numbers, (optional) scientific notation.

Resources Example Lesson Plan

UNIT 3: Algebraic Expressions & Equations
~25 days

UNDER CONSTRUCTION

REVIEW: order of operaitons, inverse operations, solving one-step equations.
FOCUS: creating algebraic expressions & equations to represent real world problems, solving 2-step equations, creating linear equations from tables, solving multi-step equations using combining like terms & distributive properties.

Resources Example Lesson Plan

UNIT 4: Algebraic Inequalities
~8 days

UNDER CONSTRUCTION

REVIEW: numberline ordering of rational numbers, inequality substitution.
FOCUS: writing & graphing inequalities, solving 1-step inequalities with rational numbers.

Resources Example Lesson Plan

UNIT 5: Angles
~8 days

UNDER CONSTRUCTION

REVIEW: acute, right, obtuse, and straight angles; anatomy of angles and triangles
FOCUS: supplementary, complementary, vertical, and adjacent angles; missing angle calculations, similar triangles, and proportional measures

Resources Example Lesson Plan

UNIT 6: Proportions
~14 days

UNDER CONSTRUCTION

REVIEW: ratios, rates & unit rates.
FOCUS: writing proportions, solving proportions using proportions, cross products property, using proportions to interpret circle graphs.

Resources Example Lesson Plan

UNIT 7: Percent
~10 days

UNDER CONSTRUCTION

REVIEW: fraction, decimal & percent conversion & ordering.
FOCUS: solving percent problems using the percent equation or percent proportion, solving percent change problems.

Resources Example Lesson Plan

UNIT 8: Probability
~10 days

UNDER CONSTRUCTION

REVIEW: N/A.
FOCUS: probability, outomces, complementary events, experimental & theoretical probability, compound probability, independent & dependent events.

Resources Example Lesson Plan

UNIT 9: Geometric Measures
~21 days

UNDER CONSTRUCTION

REVIEW: calculating vertical & horizontal distances on coordinate planes, perimeter & area of polygons & composite shapes, volume of rectangular prisms.
FOCUS: circumference & area of circles, perimeter & area of semicircles, perimeter & area of composite figures, missing lengths of composite shapes, surface area & volume of prisms.

Resources Example Lesson Plan

UNIT 10: Data Analysis
~10 days

UNDER CONSTRUCTION

REVIEW: measures of central tendency, data displays.
FOCUS: interpreting data displays, identifying souirces of bias, samples & populations, comparing populations.

Resources Example Lesson Plan

UNIT 11: Linear Equations
~10 days

UNDER CONSTRUCTION

REVIEW: substitution into equations.
FOCUS: graphing linear equations, slope of a line, graphing linear equations in slope-intercept form, writing equations in slope-intercept form.
